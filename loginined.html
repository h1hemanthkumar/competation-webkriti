About Us

Harmonic Tunes, one of the premier music streaming platforms in New York, is a well-organized digital service strategically located in the heart of Manhattan. Hemanth and Karthik, the co-founders of Harmonic Tunes, launched the platform in August 2010, with the vision of creating a universal soundscape. Harmonic Tunes has excelled in building a dedicated, skilled, and innovative team of professionals over the past nearly 14 years.

Not only that, but there is a passion at work that has always allowed Harmonic Tunes to make music streaming and discovery affordable and accessible to the general public. This has greatly aided users in discovering new music, connecting with artists, simplifying listening experiences, and creating dynamic music-sharing networks, allowing the platform to function as a virtual concert hall.

At Harmonic Tunes, we offer a seamless and user-friendly music streaming experience that gives your musical taste a prominent presence in the digital realm. As a result, our users praise us as the best music streaming platform in New York.
